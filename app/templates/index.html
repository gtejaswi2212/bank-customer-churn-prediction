How It Works

  1. 1 Customer and transaction data is cleaned and encoded.
  2. 2 Multiple models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) are trained and compared.
  3. 3 The best model is selected and evaluated on a held-out test set.
  4. 4 Predictions are turned into risk categories and retention recommendations.

Why This Project Matters

Reducing churn directly improves revenue and lifetime value. This end-to-end pipeline demonstrates strong ML fundamentals (preprocessing, class imbalance, evaluation), explainability, and a production-style web app—exactly the kind of work that drives business impact.

Tech Stack

Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, imbalanced-learn, Flask, Jinja2, Gunicorn. Optional: SHAP for explainability.

Challenges & Learnings

  • Class imbalance handled with SMOTE and metrics (precision/recall/F1, ROC-AUC) chosen for skewed targets.
  • Reproducible pipeline: fixed splits, saved encoders/scalers and feature list for consistent train and inference.
  • Business-facing output: risk bands and retention actions so stakeholders can act on predictions.
